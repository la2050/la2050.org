K-8 chronic absenteeism learn
In 2019, 18.2 percent of K-8 students in LAUSD were considered "chronically absent," meaning they were absent for 10 percent or more of the instructional days they were enrolled.

Source: LAUSD (via the California School Dashboard)
Our target:
Less than one percent of children will be chronically absent from school.
