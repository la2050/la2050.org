Dramatic Results

Marketing Intern

Employment type
Part-Time
Posted
6 months ago
Location
Signal Hill - Los Angeles
Description

This is a part-time temporary position. Reports to the Impact & Data Lead. 

Salary: $15/hourly; minimum 15 hours a week with a total of 400 hours by March 1, 2021.

Workspace: This position is currently remote, but tech support can be provided. 

NOTE: Applicants must be currently enrolled as a university / college student. 

Description 

Our current social media channels are lacking engagement and our audience growth is stagnant. We’re not looking for someone to maintain the status quo, this internship will be a hands-on opportunity to research, experiment and create content to share on our digital channels with support across all departments. You’ll learn in a fail forward environment how to go from start to finish and refine your process while creating data-driven work to add to your portfolio. Expect to try on different ‘hats’ in marketing, visual and oral communications, etc. 
 

Prospective Projects: 

  • Assist with ideation, planning, sourcing, and developing content for digital platforms (social media, blogs, newsletters etc.) 
  • Monitoring efforts and engaging audiences (learn how to reflect and make adjustments based on findings and feedback) 
  • Collaborate across departments for insights on effective communication for different audiences 
  • Organize and clean audience contacts list for use across platforms. 

 

Qualifications 

Candidates should be interested in some or all the following: Marketing, Market research, visual and oral communications, storytelling, social media, design, creative writing, journalism, social justice and equity.
 

To apply

Interested applicants should send a resume, cover letter, and one recommendation to  [email protected]. Questions can be addressed to Ryan Nuckolls, Director of Operations via the admin email.
