Hire LA's Youth Summer 2020

Employment type
Part-Time, Full-Time, Internship
Posted
7 months ago
Location
Los Angeles
Description

The Hire LA's Youth Program of the City of Los Angeles is now accepting applications for jobs this summer! If you are between the ages of 14 and 24 and live in the City of Los Angeles, you may be eligible to apply. 

This summer, the program will provide job opportunities to youth and young adults, placing them in a variety of industries, including restoration services, entertainment, tech, healthcare, government, information technology, and more.

Jobs pay at least $15 an hour.

This summer's jobs include:

  • ALL-VIRTUAL job opportunities from the safety of your home — packed with an engaging online curriculum on career and entrepreneurship training, resume writing, leadership coaching, and project-based activities. (Available to youth ages 14 - 24)
  • IN-PERSON job opportunities with health and safety protocols in place — focused on economic recovery and restoration, environmental and beautification projects, and other essential and traditional work as Los Angeles continues to re-open. The program will also provide you with the tools you need to navigate the current job market and find lasting employment in 2020 and beyond. (Available to youth ages 18 - 24)
To apply

To learn more about the program and to apply, visit: https://www.hirelayouth.com/
