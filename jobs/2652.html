Lil Dicky

Director of Community Relations and Social Responsibility

Employment type
Full-Time
Posted
7 months ago
Location
Los Angeles
Description

Client (rapper, actor) is seeking a Director of Community Relations and Social Responsibility to lead all social impact initiatives.
 
This person will be the client's go-to for everything cause/social responsibility related. They will interface with community and organization leaders, maintain relationships for the client and proactively look for social impact opportunities for the client to participate in. Candidate must also keep the client abreast on current events/social issues so that he is well informed when speaking or utilizing social media. This person will also oversee all of the client's current social impact initiatives in the space. Must have an understanding of key issues that matter to the client (see below) as well as an understanding of key local and federal races as well as ballot measures and policies that impact issues the client cares about – and be able to recommend where the client can have the most impact, and ideally have those political relationships to move on.


Qualifications & skills we are looking for:

  • Bachelor's degree in related field; MBA/JD a plus
  • Must have experience organizing and leading programs in one or more of the topics below
  • Great general knowledge of some of the bigger societal issues facing the US
  • Able to tap into a strong network as needed
  • Strong writer
  • Able to distill information easily - client will want regular white papers, news summaries, etc. 
  • Experience working with high-level clients a plus 
  • Pro-active go-getter and able to take on special projects as needed 
  • Understanding of key political races and initiatives as related to key topics (below) 
  • Utilize social media in terms of messaging, strategies, and creating assets

 

Key issues for client: 
Climate change, voter registration/education, race relations in America, LGBTQ+. women's empowerment
