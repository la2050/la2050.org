Venice Arts

Teaching Artist - Photographer

Employment type
Part-Time
Posted
3 months ago
Location
Los Angeles
Description

The ideal candidate is a graduate of a photography program with experience in documentary, photojournalism, and/or street photography, strongly preferred.  The candidate should have at least two years experience teaching photography to young people (both middle- and high-school strongly preferred); experience teaching at the beginning to advanced levels, a strong positive. Must be proficient at building a syllabus with sequenced lesson plans and demonstrate an interactive, experiential, and project-based approach. The candidate should have knowledge of historic and contemporary photographers and photographic practices. An understanding of the educational equity needs of low-income youth a plus, as is being bilingual (Spanish/English). 

Must be available to teach Wednesdays from 3:30 – 6:30 pm 
February 3– April 28, 2021.

To apply

Please email your résumé, along with a cover letter ('Photo Faculty' in the subject line), to [email protected]  Please, no phone inquiries or walk–ins.  We are sorry, but because we receive significant interest in these opportunities, we are not able to respond to every artist’s submission.
