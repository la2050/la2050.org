Reports

LA2050 conducts research and reporting to inform Angelenos about the status of our region and the progress toward the LA2050 goals. The reports measure indicators, goals, and metrics to track the quality of life in Los Angeles. The reports also analyze the work of LA2050 grantees and the trends and impact of submissions to the My LA2050 Grants Challenge and #LA2050Listens community conversation series.

#PartyatthePolls Report

#PartyatthePolls was a series of events where we asked Angelenos for event ideas to improve voter engagement in the 2016 June primary election. Eight proposals received $3500 each to pilot events and improve voter turnout.
